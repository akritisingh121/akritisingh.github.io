Hi! My name is Akriti Singh, and I am currently a junior at UC Berkeley. I am studying Computer Science and Statistics, and I am interested in the intersection of those fields. Last year, I interned at Riptide as a data science intern, and this summer I will be interning at Amazon in Seattle as a software development intern.

I'm also very passionate about design, and I love spending time making aesthetically pleasing things, whether it's doodling calligraphy in the margins of my notebooks or editing photos to make them match my Instagram theme.
