Hi! My name is Akriti Singh, and I am currently a senior at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and Data Science. Last summer I interned at Amazon in Seattle on the Alexa SmartHome team as a software development intern.

I'm also very passionate about design, and I love spending time making aesthetically pleasing things, whether it's doodling calligraphy in the margins of my notebooks or editing photos to make them match my Instagram theme. I have a background in human centered design, and I'm currently a product design consultant at Berkeley Innovation, a human centered design consultancy at UC Berkeley.

This semester, I am a TA for Data 8, a class at UC Berkeley called Foundations of Data Science. I am also the publicity officer for the Association of Women in EE&CS (AWE), an organization that promotes diversity and inclusivity in the CS community at Cal.

In my free time I enjoy making things, including (but not limited to) graphic art, website designs, and cool DIY crafts. You can download my resume here, and feel free to contact me to chat about anything!
